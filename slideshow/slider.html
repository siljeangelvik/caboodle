HAPPY @ WORK

Companies with happy employees outperform the competition by 20%

Make the working space more fun and encourage your employees to let their inner child out!

67% of full-time employees with access to free food at work are "extremely" or "very" happy at their current job

Free snacks or lunch at work are a good opportunity to show your employees that you care about their wellbeing

Close work friendships boost employee satisfaction by 50%

Next time you see your employees giggling around the water cooler, don't scold them but let them build friendships at work.

A "Wall of Fame" can boost up your employee engagement

Having a "Wall of Fame" in your office can be a fun idea. Don't only focus on standard accomplishments but also include fun ones, such as "the most coffee drunk in one day" or "the grumpiest person on Monday".

Theme days increase fun at work

Organizing theme days can bring a lot of joy at work, for example: Hawaiian T-Shirt Thursday or Favourite Superhero Friday.
